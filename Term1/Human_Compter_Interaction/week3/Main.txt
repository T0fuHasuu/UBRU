@ Interaction is the process of being able to communication with machine and digital through interface.

@ 2 types of interaction :
_ Indirect : using interface to communicate.
_ direct : putting into command.

@ Interaction can be received through touch screen, microphone, mouse etc....

@ Interaction should be include :
_ Domain : field of wanted studies
_ Goal : what it wants
_ Task : procedure in order to make it
_ Intention : For what purpose.
