# Vision and Hearing: 5 senses of individual as human being.
_ Input-Output Channels: Vision, hearing, touch, smell, and taste contribute to information intake.

_ Human Memory: Short-term and long-term memory store and retrieve information.
_ Human Processing: Decision-making, reasoning, and problem-solving are complex cognitive functions.
